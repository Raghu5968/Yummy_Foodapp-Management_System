Food delivery apps are third-party delivery services hosted on mobile applications that restaurants or retailers partner with to showcase their menu and food offerings, allowing customers to order food and get it delivered to their doorstep. Whether it's fast food or groceries, food delivery apps have revolutionized the restaurant and food delivery industry as they offer customers a convenient way to order food from the comfort of their homes.

The terms food safety and food quality can sometimes be confusing. Food safety refers to all those hazards, whether chronic or acute, that may make food injurious to the health of the consumer. It is not negotiable. Quality includes all other attributes that influence a product's value to the consumer.
